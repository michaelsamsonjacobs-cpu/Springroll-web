# Franchise Launch Workflow

White-label and license Springroll to companies as a franchise or SaaS reseller.

## Franchise vs. Licensing Decision

| Model | Best For | Complexity | Revenue |
|-------|----------|------------|---------|
| **SaaS Reseller** | Volume, hands-off | Low | Monthly recurring |
| **White-Label** | Agencies, consultants | Medium | License + rev share |
| **Franchise** | Full business system | High | Fees + royalties |

---

## Step 1: Prepare Your Offering

### White-Label Package Contents
- [ ] Customizable branding (logo, colors, name)
- [ ] Subdomain or custom domain support
- [ ] Partner admin dashboard
- [ ] Training materials and documentation
- [ ] Support escalation process
- [ ] Pricing framework for their clients

### Franchise Package Additions
- [ ] Operations manual
- [ ] Marketing materials and playbooks
- [ ] Territory definitions
- [ ] Performance requirements
- [ ] Training certification program

---

## Step 2: Legal Requirements

### For Licensing/White-Label
- [ ] Master License Agreement
- [ ] Terms of Service for end users
- [ ] Data Processing Agreement (DPA)
- [ ] SLA (Service Level Agreement)
- [ ] Payment terms and invoicing

### For Franchise (Additional)

> [!CAUTION]
> Franchising is heavily regulated. Consult a franchise attorney.

- [ ] Franchise Disclosure Document (FDD)
- [ ] State registrations (14 states require)
- [ ] Franchise Agreement
- [ ] Operations manual (legally binding)
- [ ] Territory rights documentation

---

## Step 3: Pricing Models

### SaaS Reseller
```
Partner pays: $X/seat/month (wholesale)
Partner charges: $Y/seat/month (retail)
Partner keeps: $Y - $X margin
```

### White-Label License
```
Setup fee: $5,000 - $25,000
Monthly platform fee: $500 - $2,000
Revenue share: 10-30% of partner revenue
```

### Franchise
```
Initial franchise fee: $25,000 - $100,000
Ongoing royalty: 5-8% of gross revenue
Marketing fund: 1-2% of gross revenue
Technology fee: $500 - $1,000/month
```

---

## Step 4: Partner Onboarding

### Technical Setup
1. Create partner tenant/instance
2. Configure branding (logo, colors, domain)
3. Set up admin accounts
4. Connect payment processing
5. Migrate any existing data

### Training Program
1. **Product Training** — Features, workflows
2. **Sales Training** — Demo, objection handling
3. **Support Training** — Troubleshooting, escalation
4. **Marketing Training** — Positioning, materials

### Go-Live Checklist
- [ ] Technical setup complete
- [ ] Training completed and certified
- [ ] Legal agreements signed
- [ ] First invoice processed
- [ ] Support channels established

---

## Step 5: Partner Success

### Key Metrics to Track
| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Partner activation rate | 80%+ |
| Partner churn | <10% annual |
| End-user seats per partner | Growth |
| Support ticket volume | Decreasing |
| NPS (partner satisfaction) | 50+ |

### Ongoing Support
- Monthly partner calls
- Quarterly business reviews
- Partner portal with resources
- Slack/Discord community
- Annual partner conference

---

## Step 6: Scale the Program

### Partner Tiers
| Tier | Requirements | Benefits |
|------|--------------|----------|
| **Starter** | Signed agreement | Basic support, standard pricing |
| **Silver** | 10+ seats, certified | Priority support, better margin |
| **Gold** | 50+ seats, references | Dedicated CSM, co-marketing |
| **Platinum** | 200+ seats | Custom terms, roadmap input |

### Expansion Strategies
1. **Geographic** — Country/region exclusivity
2. **Vertical** — Industry specialization (legal, finance)
3. **Channel** — MSPs, system integrators

---

## Legal Resources

| Document | Purpose |
|----------|---------|
| Reseller Agreement | Simple distribution rights |
| White-Label License | Branding + customization rights |
| Franchise Agreement | Full business system license |
| FDD | Required franchise disclosure |
| Territory Agreement | Exclusive geographic rights |

> [!TIP]
> Start with reseller/licensing before franchising. It's faster and less regulated.
